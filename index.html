BostonBIAS

BostonBIAS is a community established to unite and empower Bio-image Analysts located in the greater Boston area. We are committed to fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and innovation. Inspired by the principles of global organizations like Glo-BIAS, we offer a platform where Bio-image Analysts can engage in discussions, share insights, and embark on exciting cooperative projects. Our goal is to accumulate the experiences and needs of BIAS users, from core facilities to collaborative research ventures, and help define the evolving role of bioimage analysts in the Boston scientific landscape. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a budding enthusiast, BostonBIAS welcomes all who are eager to explore and contribute to the world of bio-image analysis.
