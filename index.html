Welcome to
BostonBIAS!

BostonBIAS is a community for Bio-image Analysts in the Boston area, aimed at promoting collaboration, knowledge sharing, and innovation. Taking cues from organizations like Glo-BIAS, we provide a space for analysts to discuss, share insights, and start joint projects. Our objective is to gather input from BIAS users across various settings, from core facilities to research collaborations, to better understand and shape the role of bioimage analysts in Boston's scientific community. BostonBIAS is open to everyone interested in bio-image analysis, regardless of their experience level.
